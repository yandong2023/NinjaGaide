Ninja Gaiden 4: Yakumo's New Adventure

Coming in Fall 2025, Ninja Gaiden 4 marks a bold new direction for the legendary action series. For the first time in the franchise's history, players will step into the role of Yakumo, a mysterious new protagonist with ties to the Dragon Lineage.

New Combat System

The game introduces the revolutionary Bloodraven Form system, allowing Yakumo to transform and adapt his combat style mid-battle. This dynamic system brings unprecedented depth to the series' already renowned combat mechanics.

Enhanced Movement

Yakumo's agility introduces new traversal options, including the ability to use wires for swinging and rail-sliding. These additions create more vertical gameplay opportunities while maintaining the series' signature fast-paced action.

Next-Gen Graphics

Built on the latest gaming technology, Ninja Gaiden 4 showcases stunning visuals with real-time ray tracing, detailed character models, and fluid animations running at 60 FPS on current-gen consoles.

Key Features:

  • New protagonist Yakumo with unique abilities
  • Revolutionary Bloodraven Form combat system
  • Enhanced movement mechanics
  • Next-gen graphics and performance
  • Deep, engaging storyline
